Questions

Membership
FAQ

How do I join LAPEQ? +
The Free tier is open to everyone — simply fill in the contact form and our team will confirm your membership and mail your physical card. Silver, Gold, and Black memberships require an application and a brief onboarding call with our concierge team to ensure we can serve you properly.
What is the difference between Silver and Gold? +
Silver gives you access to the core LAPEQ concierge service — travel, hotel, restaurant, and car hire — during business hours with a shared concierge team. Gold upgrades you to a dedicated concierge manager, faster response times, VIP-level access, personal assistant support, medical concierge, and our full diaspora and business service suite.
Is the Black membership truly invitation-only? +
Yes. The Black tier is kept to a strictly limited number of members to ensure that every Black member receives a genuinely unlimited, fully personalised level of service. Current Gold members in good standing may be invited to apply. Applications from outside the membership are considered on a case-by-case basis.
Can I upgrade my membership at any time? +
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Silver or Gold at any time by contacting your LAPEQ concierge or reaching out through the contact form. Upgrades to Black require a separate application process and are subject to availability.
Does LAPEQ offer corporate membership? +
Yes. Corporate memberships are available for teams, firms, and companies who want to extend LAPEQ access to their staff as a premium benefit. Corporate accounts include a dedicated account manager, negotiated pricing based on team size, and access to our Corporate Pairing programme for executive networking. Contact us for a tailored proposal.
Which cities does LAPEQ currently operate in? +
LAPEQ currently operates in Abuja, Lagos, Port Harcourt, and Jos. Diaspora support services are available for members based anywhere in the world, with Nigeria-side coordination handled through our local concierge network.
