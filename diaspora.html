Trusted Eyes in Nigeria

When you're living in London, Houston, Toronto, or Dubai — your property, your assets, and your family are still in Nigeria. You deserve to know the truth about what's happening there, verified by professionals bound by law to tell you exactly that.

Project Trust is LAPEQ's flagship diaspora service: a structured, recurring property and asset oversight program delivered by dedicated ground agents, documented with photo and video evidence, and reported to you on a monthly cadence with absolute confidentiality.

"We don't just tell you everything is fine.
We show you — with evidence."

Every Project Trust report is verified, signed off by a senior LAPEQ Diaspora Manager, and delivered as a formatted document you can access on any device. Issues are flagged the moment they're discovered, not buried in a monthly summary.

The Process

How Project Trust Works

From your first consultation to your first report — here's exactly what happens when you enrol in Project Trust.

I

Intake Call

You speak with a Diaspora Manager who maps your property, assets, and any concerns. Everything is covered by NDA before you say a word.

II

Agent Assignment

A dedicated ground agent is assigned to your location. They are background-checked, trained, and bound by a personal NDA to your account only.

III

First Inspection

Your agent conducts a full initial inspection within 72 hours, establishing a baseline condition record for all assets on your portfolio.

IV

Monthly Reports

Every month, a formatted Project Trust Report is delivered to you — with photos, condition ratings, incident notes, and a summary from your Diaspora Manager.

V

Real-Time Alerts

Any issue flagged between reports is communicated to you immediately, with a WhatsApp + email summary and a recommended action plan from our team.

Questions

Common Questions

If you don't find your answer here, speak with a Diaspora Manager — we respond within 2 hours on WhatsApp.

How does LAPEQ ensure agents are trustworthy?
+
All Project Trust agents are background-checked, reference-verified, and trained by LAPEQ before assignment. Each agent signs a personal Non-Disclosure Agreement that is specific to your account. They are also not aware of your international address or personal circumstances — only the property details they need to inspect.
Can I enrol multiple properties in different cities?
+
Yes. LAPEQ has ground agents in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Akwa-Ibom, Enugu, Kaduna, and several other states. Multi-city portfolios are typically managed under our Guardian or Sentinel plans. Contact us to discuss your specific portfolio.
What happens if there's an emergency between monthly visits?
+
If your caretaker, a neighbour, or any other contact reports an issue, your Diaspora Manager is alerted and dispatches your ground agent within 24 hours (Sentinel plan: same day). You receive a full emergency incident report with photos, a situation assessment, and recommended next steps.
Is Project Trust only for Nigerians in the diaspora?
+
Primarily, yes — it was designed for Nigerians living abroad who need trusted eyes at home. However, we also work with Nigerians based in Nigeria who travel frequently for business, or who have properties in cities they don't reside in.
How do I receive the reports?
+
Your Project Trust Report is delivered as a formatted PDF to your email, with a WhatsApp notification when it's ready. Reports are also stored in your secure LAPEQ portal where you can view all historical reports, photos, and incident logs going back 12+ months.
Can I pause or cancel at any time?
+
Yes. Project Trust is a rolling monthly service with no long-term commitment required (though discounts are available for 6 and 12-month pre-payments). You can pause your plan if you return to Nigeria for an extended period, or cancel with 30 days notice.
What cities does LAPEQ Diaspora cover?
+
We currently offer full Project Trust coverage in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, with partial coverage (available on request) in Enugu, Kaduna, Ibadan, Benin City, Akwa-Ibom, and Delta State. Coverage is expanding — speak with us about your specific location.
