Questions

Frequently
Asked

What exactly does LAPEQ do? +
LAPEQ is a premium concierge and lifestyle management company. We handle hotel bookings, flight arrangements, restaurant reservations, event access, car hire, personal assistant services, medical concierge, diaspora support, business assistance, and more across Abuja, Lagos, and Port Harcourt .
How do I get started with LAPEQ? +
Simply fill in the contact form below, or reach out via WhatsApp or phone. Our team will respond within hours to understand your needs and recommend the most suitable membership tier or service for you.
Is there a free membership option? +
Yes. The LAPEQ Free tier is open to everyone and costs nothing. You receive a physical LAPEQ membership card mailed to you, a monthly lifestyle digest, curated city guides, and access to free community events. No subscription, free forever.
Which cities does LAPEQ operate in? +
LAPEQ currently operates in Abuja, Lagos and Port Harcourt. Diaspora support services are available for members based anywhere in the world, with all Nigeria-side coordination handled through our local concierge teams.
How confidential are my requests? +
Absolute confidentiality is a core LAPEQ standard. Every request, preference, and piece of personal information is treated with the highest level of discretion. Our team operates under strict confidentiality protocols at all membership tiers.
What is the Black membership? +
The Black tier is LAPEQ's invitation-only level, kept to a strictly limited number of members to ensure genuinely unlimited, fully personalised service. It includes 24/7 dedicated concierge, unlimited requests, private security arrangements, full lifestyle management, and exclusive invitation-only experiences.
