FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is a concierge service? +
A concierge service provides personalized lifestyle management and support — handling travel, reservations, access, logistics, and personal tasks to save you time and eliminate stress. At LAPEQ, we go beyond task completion to curate complete, seamless experiences.
Is LAPEQ only for high-net-worth individuals? +
No. While we offer premium and VIP services, our flexible membership packages — including a completely free tier — make LAPEQ accessible to professionals, international visitors, and individuals seeking quality service at every level.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking in advance for major events, travel arrangements, and exclusive access requests. However, we also accommodate urgent and last-minute requests whenever possible — our team is always ready to assist.
Do you offer 24/7 support? +
Yes. Our concierge team is available around the clock. Gold and Black members receive priority access to a dedicated concierge manager at all hours.
Are my requests handled confidentially? +
Absolutely. Discretion and client privacy are core values at LAPEQ. All requests and personal information are handled with strict confidentiality — especially for Black membership and corporate clients.
Do you handle private security arrangements? +
Yes. For clients who require it, LAPEQ can coordinate private security arrangements with trusted, vetted professionals. This service is available primarily to Gold and Black membership clients, handled with complete discretion.
