Frequently Asked

Questions We
Answer Every Day

What exactly does a LAPEQ concierge do?
+
Your LAPEQ concierge is a dedicated professional who manages your requests — from hotel bookings and restaurant reservations to complex arrangements like construction oversight, private dining, and executive introductions. You communicate your needs; we handle everything else, from initial arrangement through to final confirmation.
How does LAPEQ handle my privacy?
+
Every LAPEQ team member signs a Non-Disclosure Agreement as a condition of employment. All client data is stored on encrypted systems and is never shared, sold, or referenced outside of the direct concierge relationship.
What is Project Trust and who is it for?
+
Project Trust is LAPEQ's independent construction oversight service for Nigerians in the diaspora and clients who cannot be physically present on their building sites. We deploy an independent inspector every week — with no relationship to your contractor — who delivers date-stamped photos, drone footage, and a plain-English Friday report directly to you.
Which membership tier should I start with?
+
Our Free tier provides community access, the LAPEQ card, and partner discounts. Silver is ideal for regular travel and hospitality management. Gold unlocks the full service suite. Black is invitation-only with a dedicated personal manager. Contact us to discuss your requirements before selecting.
Can LAPEQ assist me if I live outside Nigeria?
+
Yes. Our diaspora services are specifically designed for Nigerians living abroad. We manage property oversight, construction monitoring, family liaison, and arrival coordination remotely. Many of our Gold and Black members are based in the UK, US, UAE, and Canada.
